About Us

LIPAMON is a bilingual bookstore located at Douala, more precisely at Kotto, near by the sub-prefecture of Kotto. It has been created in 2015. LIPAMON is a family business head up by the mother of the family, KAKMENI NGAGOUM JUDITH ARMELLE. With more than a hundred loyal clients, LIPAMON intend to be a reference in its activity sector.
